08 — Quick Wins

First 30 Days

01
Set Up All Profiles

Use the same handle, same profile photo (logo), and same cover image across all platforms. Link all accounts in your bio where possible.

02
Post 3 Foundation Posts

Before inviting anyone to follow you, have at least 3 quality posts live — an intro, a facility showcase, and an upcoming event teaser.

03
Tag Local Teams & Partners

Tag sports teams, promoters, and local businesses in posts. Their followers discover you, and you build community goodwill fast.

04
Run a Follow + Win Promo

Launch a free ticket giveaway: "Follow us + tag a friend to win 2 tickets." Low cost, massive organic reach, and instant follower growth.

05
Reply to Every Comment

In the first 30 days, reply to EVERY comment and DM. The algorithm rewards engagement and fans remember when a brand speaks to them.

06
Shoot a Hero Reel

Invest in one high-quality 30-second cinematic reel of the arena. This is your pinned post and the piece of content that makes people say "wow."
